Breast Cancer Detection

Breast cancer is the most prevalent cancer among Indian women,
contributing to nearly 30% of new cases and over 24% of related deaths.
"three" in every "five" people in India die following a cancer diagnosis,
with women bearing a disproportionate burden.

Challenges Women Face in Early Detection

  • Fear of discomfort and exposure in mammography.
  • Lack of awareness about self-examinations and screening importance.
  • Social stigma associated with discussing breast health.

How Our Model Helps

Our AI-based thermal image-based breast cancer detection model provides a non-invasive, radiation-free, and comfortable alternative to traditional mammography.

  • ✔ Uses Thermal Imaging: Eliminates the discomfort and exposure of traditional screening.
  • ✔ Fast and Accessible: Can be used for preliminary screening, even in remote areas.
  • ✔ Early Detection: Helps in identifying potential cases early, encouraging women to consult doctors sooner.
  • ✔ AI-Powered Accuracy: Differentiates between NORMAL vs SICK and further classifies Benign vs Malignant cases, aiding doctors in decision-making.

By providing a comfortable and stigma-free screening option, our model empowers Indian women to prioritize their health, seek medical consultation early, and improve survival rates through timely intervention.
This technology aims to bridge the gap in early detection and make breast cancer screening more accessible and acceptable for every woman in India.
